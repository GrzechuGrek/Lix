** LOST **

You and the remaining travellers
step through the portal.

The sun is shining and we see that we
are back on our favorite beach. HOME!

But wait, you don't recognize anyone
among the crowds at the beach.
Did time stand still for us while we
were gone? How many generations
have passed on our own planet?

You then notice imperfections in the
scenery. Where did that rock come
from? We need to get to the bottom
of this. Let's explore...
